What is ASU Marketplace ?

ASU Marketplace is an app designed by students for students. Our main goal of the app is to make buying and selling items for students living on any of ASUs campuses as easy as possible.


The app works as simple as creating an account with your ASU email so we can verify your a student and then you're in. It's as simple as that. You can then post your own items for sale or find and purchase other peoples items that catch your intrest. The best part is you can view listing depending on which campus you're staying at so if you don't have a car no problem since you're of walking distance of eachother.

A Safe Place For Students

ASU Marketplace makes sure to keep your personal information safe from others. We even don't ask you for your exact location all we need is the campus you stay at.

Get Ready for the Year

One of our goals is to make it easy for students to by second hand items from eachother. These can range from previous class textbooks to an older laptop that you no longer use.

Meet new People

We are a open marketplace. So students are more then welcome to post their own services for sale. If they have their own products to promote or even sell tutoring services.
